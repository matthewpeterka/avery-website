Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about my shopping recommendations

How do you choose your top picks?

+

I personally test and research every product I recommend. My selection process includes: reading hundreds of reviews, testing products myself, considering value for money, and ensuring they meet my high quality standards. I only recommend items I truly believe in.

Do you earn commission from the links?

+

Yes, some of the links on my site are affiliate links, which means I may earn a small commission if you make a purchase through them. However, this doesn't affect the price you pay, and I only recommend products I genuinely love and believe in. My recommendations are always honest and unbiased.

How often do you update your recommendations?

+

I update my top picks regularly, typically every month. I'm constantly testing new products and keeping up with the latest releases. When I find something amazing, I can't wait to share it! Follow me on social media for real-time updates and new discoveries.

Can I request specific product recommendations?

+

Absolutely! I love hearing from my community about what they're looking for. You can reach out to me on Instagram or TikTok with your requests. I'll research and test products in that category and share my findings with everyone.

What if I don't like a product you recommended?

+

While I do my best to recommend products that work for most people, everyone's preferences and needs are different. Most of the products I recommend come with good return policies. If something doesn't work for you, I'd love to hear your feedback so I can improve my recommendations.

Do you only recommend expensive products?

+

Not at all! I believe in value over price. I recommend products across all price points, from budget-friendly finds to investment pieces. My focus is always on quality and whether a product delivers on its promises, regardless of the price tag.

How can I stay updated with your latest recommendations?

+

The best way to stay updated is to follow me on Instagram and TikTok, where I share daily recommendations and behind-the-scenes content. You can also subscribe to my newsletter for weekly roundups of my latest finds and exclusive deals.

What categories of products do you focus on?

+

I cover a wide range of categories including beauty, skincare, home & lifestyle, fashion, tech gadgets, fitness, and wellness products. I'm always expanding into new categories based on what my community is interested in and what I'm personally excited about.

Still Have Questions?

I'm here to help! Reach out to me on social media or send me a message.
